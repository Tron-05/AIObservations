AI Observations by Professor Tony Hinton

Soon, it will become increasingly apparent in competitive markets that using AI for work tasks is not just an option but a necessity. As a result, we can expect our boss to ask why we didn't use AI to complete a task.

- Professor Tony Hinton, October 2023

To benefit from AI, people have quickly adapted to using computers for approximations rather than the exactness of outputs we had before.

- Professor Tony Hinton, October 2023

Today, when the Internet is down, most businesses cannot operate. In the near future, when AI systems fail, it will have a similar impact.

- Professor Tony Hinton, October 2023
